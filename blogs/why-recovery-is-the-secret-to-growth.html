Why recovery is the secret to growth

Schedule dedicated rest days into your routine, just as you would workout days.

Category:
Recovery
Published on:
June 12, 2025
Chris Walker
Rescuer instructor

Introduction

Quality sleep, a cornerstone of recovery, is when the body releases the majority of its growth hormone, which is vital for tissue repair and muscle development.

Apply Progressive Overload

The key to growth is gradual, consistent overload — increasing resistance or intensity over time.

  • - Adding small weight increments weekly
  • - Increasing reps or sets gradually
  • - Reducing rest periods strategically
  • - Mixing tempo (slow eccentric reps for more muscle activation)
"Healing takes courage, and we all have courage, even if we have to dig a little to find it."

Key Takeaways

  • - Master your form before loading heavy
  • - Use progressive overload to growth
  • - Balance training with proper recovery
  • - Fuel your body with smart nutrition
